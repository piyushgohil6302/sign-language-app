Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

🔒 Our Commitment to Privacy

SignLive AI is built with privacy at its core. We believe that accessibility technology should never come at the cost of your personal data. This page explains how we protect your information.

📷 Camera Data

Our sign language detection feature requires access to your device's camera. Here's what you need to know:

  • Local Processing Only: All camera data is processed entirely within your browser. Video frames are analyzed by MediaPipe AI running locally on your device.
  • No Recording: We never record, store, or save any video frames from your camera.
  • No Transmission: Camera data is never sent to external servers, APIs, or third parties.
  • Instant Disposal: Each video frame is analyzed and immediately discarded. No frames persist in memory beyond active detection.

🧠 AI Model Data

The AI models used for hand detection and gesture recognition:

  • Are loaded from Google's MediaPipe CDN as static files
  • Run entirely in your browser (client-side inference)
  • Do not send your gesture data or detection results to any server
  • Do not learn or adapt from your usage

📊 Analytics & Tracking

SignLive AI does not use any analytics, tracking cookies, or user monitoring tools. We do not:

  • Track your usage patterns
  • Store browsing history
  • Use cookies for tracking
  • Integrate with advertising networks
  • Collect any personally identifiable information

🌐 Third-Party Resources

The only external resources loaded by this application are:

  • Google Fonts: Typography loaded from Google's CDN (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • MediaPipe Models: AI model files loaded from jsDelivr CDN

🛡️ Security Measures

  • No user accounts or authentication required
  • No personal data collected or stored
  • Application works offline once models are cached
  • All external connections use HTTPS

📩 Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please reach out through our project repository.
